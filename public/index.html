What is WatchTogether?

WatchTogether is a free online watch party platform that lets you watch movies and TV shows together online with your friends, family, or partner — no matter where they are in the world. One person hosts by sharing a video link, friends join with a room code, and everyone watches in perfect sync with a live chat sidebar.

How to Watch Together Online — Step by Step

  1. Create an alias — Enter your name above in under 5 seconds.
  2. Create a watch room — Click "Create Room" and get a unique invite code. Share the code with friends.
  3. Load a Video — Paste a YouTube link or direct video file into the player.
  4. Watch together — Friends join instantly using the room code. Everyone watches in perfect sync. Chat and react together in real time.

Features — Everything You Need for a Perfect Virtual Movie Night

📺 Real-Time Sync Ultra-low latency NTP-style synchronization. If someone pauses or buffers, the whole room stays together automatically.
💬 Live Group Chat Chat with everyone in the room as you watch. React, comment, and share the experience just like you would sitting together on the same couch.
🔒 Private Watch Rooms Every room has a unique invite code. Only people you share the code with can join — your movie night stays private.
📱 Works on Any Device Viewers can join and chat on mobile, tablet, or desktop. Our responsive interface adapts perfectly to any screen size.
